    Daily Focus

    This sheet is meant to provide a little more structure so that you are not left thinking " okay now what?". This sheet aims to provide a concept called themed days where on one particular day of the week you are planning the content you want to post next week, on the next day you are sorting out all the logistics and so on. The point of this is to have everything organized and be more effective.

    Day of the Week Tasks
    Monday Content Planning Day - Outline videos/posts, script writing, storyboard creation.
    Tuesday Props and Equipment Planning/Purchase - Determine necessary props, equipment needs, and make purchases or rentals.
    Wednesday Filming - Record videos for the week.
    Thursday Editing - Edit the recorded videos, add effects, sounds, and finalize the content.
    Friday Publishing and Promotion - Publish the content on respective platforms and promote it on social media, email newsletters, etc.
